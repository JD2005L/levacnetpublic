Selected Work

Production systems built for real businesses.

Amrik Construction

amrik.ca

Marketing and lead-capture site for a leading Edmonton real estate development company. Built on .NET 8 Razor Pages with an MSSQL backend and an n8n contact-form workflow that classifies inquiries and routes them to the right account owner automatically.

.NET 8 Razor Pages MSSQL n8n Lead Routing

Harmani Rentals

harmanirentals.ca

Property management platform for a multi-building rental portfolio. Each building exposes a QR code that opens a tenant message form; an n8n + LLM workflow categorizes the message, summarizes it, and dispatches notifications to every property, maintenance, and security manager whose responsibility the issue touches - one of my flagship n8n AI routing pipelines. Built on .NET 8 Razor Pages with an MSSQL backend.

.NET 8 Razor Pages MSSQL n8n LLM Workflow AI Routing

ProVisionI

provisioni.ca

Two-part build for a commercial AI-surveillance company: a public marketing site at provisioni.ca, plus a full Client Portal where customers log in to view, configure and administer their surveillance services. The portal is .NET 8 Razor Pages on MSSQL with n8n workflows automating event processing, alerting, and reporting across multiple sites.

.NET 8 Razor Pages MSSQL n8n Client Portal AI Surveillance

LeadsManager CRM

Pellegrini Team Consulting

Enterprise multi-tenant CRM for financial services teams. Glass-morphism design system, V2 analytics dashboard, team management with role-based admin controls, comprehensive reporting suite, client merge handling, and feature-flag architecture. Hosted on Azure with an Azure SQL backend.

.NET 8 Razor Pages Azure Azure SQL Multi-Tenant Feature Flags

SkynDeep Esthetics

skyndeep.ca

Esthetics website, online booking, and combined business operations backend in one. Built on .NET 8 Razor Pages with a SQLite database and Identity-based authentication for staff and admin roles, plus package, service, and appointment management.

.NET 8 Razor Pages SQLite Identity Booking Operations Backend

Fraser Car Wash Analytics

Fraser Car Wash

A specialized analytics module inside the ProVisionI Client Portal serving Fraser Car Wash. Tracks operational and revenue metrics, surfaces them as live report charts in the portal, and pairs them with an AI-powered database chatbot that lets operators query their numbers in plain English.

.NET 8 MSSQL AI Chatbot Analytics Reporting
